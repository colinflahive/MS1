Cathar Trail Top Recommendations

The Restaurant

Le Rebenty, Axat, 11140

Le Rebenty

For the ultimate Gallic gastro experience, treat yourself to lunch in this quaint french restaurant located on the D119 between Quillan and Axat. The staff are extremely helpful, speack fluent English and will be delighted to ensure you have a meal you will never forget.

The Hotel

Hotel de la Cite, Carcassonne, 11000

Hotel de la Cite

For complete and utter indulgence at a very reasonable rate, treat yourself to the experience of a lifetime and stay within the walls of the ancient city of Carcassonne. The hotel offers everything that a 5 star hotel should and the staff will ensure it is a stay you will always remember.

The Activity

Gorge De Galamus, Saint-Paul-de-Fenouillet, 66220

Gorge De Galamus

This little hidden gem off the main road from Puilaurens to St-Paul offers the visitor some extraordinary scenery. Enjoy the gentle stroll down to the Hermitage De St. Antoine with it's chapel carved out of the rockface. For the more adventurous of you, enjoy a day out canyoning down the prestine waters of the Gorge with one of the local tour operators.
